GTA V TRUE MYTHS

Last updated on 7th Dec, 2024

GTA V has got very many myths of which some of these are truth while the others are false. The most common myths present in the game that are surprisingly true include; You can lose a wanted level if you exchange a car before the cops see you. You cannot pierce car tires using a knife or a sword. You can also race anyone in the game as long as you rev your tires at the lights. Chop will immediately die in case he touches the water. Chop will not fetch the ball in case it touches the water. Chop can attack Lamar though he is Chop’s former owner. Chop cannot kill any thing in the game. Fire fighters cannot stop a fire on their own truck. Fire fighters cannot catch on fire due to their fire resistant suits. A tree can catch fire in the game. You cannot tow an ATM machine. There is a map somewhere in the game that is said to be the GTA VI map. Those are some of the true myths in GTA V.
